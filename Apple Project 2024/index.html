About Samsung

Samsung Electronics is a global leader in technology, renowned for its innovative products and services. Founded in 1969 and headquartered in Suwon, South Korea, Samsung is part of the larger Samsung Group, one of the largest conglomerates in the world.

Popular Samsung Products:
1. Samsung Galaxy Smartphones:
• Galaxy S Series: Premium flagship smartphones like Galaxy S23.
• Galaxy Z Series: Innovative foldable devices like Galaxy Z Flip and Z Fold.
• Galaxy A Series: Affordable, feature-packed smartphones.
2. Televisions:
• Samsung’s QLED TVs and The Frame series combine design and performance.
• Leader in 8K display technology.
3. Wearables:
• Galaxy Watch and Galaxy Buds.
4. Home Appliances:
• Smart refrigerators, washing machines, and air conditioners.
Notable Achievements
• Samsung was the largest smartphone manufacturer in the world in 2023 by market share.
• One of the world’s top semiconductor producers, competing with companies like Intel and TSMC.
• Known for its foldable smartphone innovations, starting with the Galaxy Fold in 2019.
